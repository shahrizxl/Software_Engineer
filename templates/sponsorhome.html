About Us

At Trash and Treasure, we believe in sustainability and second chances. Use this dashboard to manage our catalog of renewed and preloved items, update pricing and promotions, and monitor customer feedback. Together, let’s create a greener future by reducing waste and promoting reuse.

Our Vision

To be the leading platform for sustainable shopping, promoting reuse and renewal as a lifestyle choice.

Our Mission

• Sustainability: Reduce landfill waste by extending the life cycle of everyday items.

• Affordability: Provide quality, affordable products to our customers.

• Community: Build a marketplace that connects buyers and sellers who value sustainability.
